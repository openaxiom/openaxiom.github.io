Making the Complicated Easy

Catalyst is compact and easy-to-use, and its object detection and weather sensors track pedestrian and vehicle traffic as well as changes in weather conditions. Once installed, the counter is self-sustaining, meaning you can spend your valuable time digging into its unique, high-quality insights to better understand traffic patterns and flow in your area.

Learn more

Real-Time Information & Easy-to-Use Dashboards

Catalyst’s sensors relay the data it collects quickly and securely to an online portal, where you can view and analyze the data for key trends and patterns. The dashboard provides information on peak hours, projections of hourly, daily, and weekly consumer activity, and on-demand PDF exports.
